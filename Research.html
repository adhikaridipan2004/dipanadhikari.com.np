"Our Website: A Mosaic of Ideas from 5 influential Sites"

In the process of building our website, we took inspiration from different websites. Five of them are listed below. Each of these sites offered unique elements that we admired and incorporated into our own design. Below we have provided the details on how each of these websites influenced us.

Amazon

Similarities


Our website has a clear and concise navigation bar, similar to the one found on Amazon. Especially in products page the color combination of the nav bar is similar to the Amazon. Moreover the overall color theme of all webpages are similar to them including the dark shade of cyan-blue theme in the home page and login page.


Differences


Despite being similar color choices the features provided by amazon are far more than on our nav-bar. Our website opts for a simpler approach, making it easier for users to find what they're looking for without feeling overwhelmed

Daraz

Similarities


As Daraz is one of the biggest e-commerce site of nepal we have taken a lot of inspirations from them. One of them being the hover effect on the products when we hover over the each products box. Similarly the concept of product box design is taken from the daraz website.


Differences


Despite having similar concepts in many thing our website has many Differences from daraz as well. One example may be the detailed description of the products. Daraz provides a separate page when clicked in the products showing detailed information of product but we provide less details of product which shows on same page sliding from the see more option.

FlipKart

Similarities


There are very few similarities of our website from the flipKart website. The idea of providing the description of the products inside the products box was taken from here. Similarly the heart option was also inserted on our site by taking inspiration from FlipKart


Differences


Despite the similarity of providing description of the product in the same product box, there is the difference of way of showing the description in our website. Our website has the feature to only show the description of the products when the customer clicks the see more option.

Walmart

Similarities


There are very few similarities of our website with walmart, one of them being the add to cart button on the product-box.


Differences


Most of the color theme as well as the design of the website is different from the walmart site.

ebay

Similarities


The color theme of the product box is mainly inspired from ebay. It has light greyish theme which is similar to what we have on our website.


Differences


Despite being similar colors for the products box, the shape, size are different from ebay. ebay has circular product-box where as we have a larger rectangle box. Moreover we provide the description of the products in the product-box but they don't.
